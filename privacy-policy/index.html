Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-05-15

At ToolTeek, accessible from toolteek.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number (e.g., when you contact us via forms).

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages (collected via cookies or analytics tools).

  • Payment Information: If applicable, through secure third-party processors (we do not store payment data ourselves).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Respond to inquiries or service requests.

  • Improve our website and services.

  • Communicate updates or marketing (only with your consent).

  • Analyze website performance using analytics tools (like Google Analytics).

3. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Provide a better browsing experience.

  • Understand how visitors interact with our website.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like:

  • Google Analytics – to track website usage.

  • Contact Forms – to collect your inquiries.

  • Payment Gateways (like PayPal or Stripe) – to process payments.

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

5. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

 

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
